Cher-ing Our Story

Where your beauty meets our excellence

For over 18 years, Cher has been the ray of style and self-care in Ahmedabad and Surat. What began as a single vision has grown into three thriving lounges — Sindhu Bhavan, Gulbai Tekra, and Ghod Dod Road in Surat.

Every visit is shaped by a team of seasoned professionals dedicated to helping you show up as your most confident self, wrapped in an atmosphere of quiet luxury.
